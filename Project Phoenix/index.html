Welcome to Project Phoenix !

What is Project Phoenix all about?

Imagine a story where you are the hero, and your choices shape the path the story takes. In this gamebook, like others, you get to decide how you react to the situations you find yourself in. This unlocks possibilities and you will face challenges and riddles along the way.

Every decision you make will steer the story in a unique direction, offering many possibilities and multiple endings to discover. You should be careful when choosing your responses, as they may have heavy consequences. Everyone will die eventually, but it may happen earlier than expected. Get ready to unlock your imagination, challenge your problem-solving skills, and experience the thrill of Project Phoenix. Are you ready to answer the call?

Project Phoenix takes place in a post-apocalyptic world, where the government collapsed after a nuclear war broke out in the year 2037. Many places on earth became polluted with radiation, causing only a few people to survive. Food and clean water have become scarce, and death could be lingering around every corner. The remaining people are often ready to kill on sight, as strangers mean danger in most cases.


How does the Story System work?

The Story System in Project Phoenix is designed to provide a dynamic and immersive experience. It combines the thrill of storytelling with the interactivity of a gamebook. As you progress through the story, you will encounter various choices and challenges that will shape the outcome. At key decision points, you will be presented with multiple options. Your choice will influence the direction of the story, determining the challenges you face, the characters you meet, and the ultimate outcome of your adventure.

The Story System also incorporates elements of chance and skill. You may need to solve puzzles, engage in strategic thinking, or utilize your knowledge to overcome obstacles and progress in the story. Pay attention to details and think carefully before making decisions, as the consequences can be significant. With multiple branching paths and endings, Project Phoenix offers a high degree of replayability. Each playthrough can uncover new storylines and reveal different facets of the world.


What are the Do's and Dont's ?

1. Embrace your decisions: You should be sure about your decisions and accept their consequences. This also counts in the case that something bad happens and you lose progress.

2. Going back pages: It may seem tempting to just go back a page after making a bad decision. However, not doing this maintains the immersion into the story and makes the whole experience better for you in the end.

3. Looking up things on the internet: You will find yourself in situations where common knowledge is required to make the right decision. The information could be found on the internet, but you should make the decision with your current knowledge. This is also how it would be, if you found yourself in that situation in real life.

4. Remember: It is still just a game. Don't reenact anything of this story in real life. Also, you may find content not suitable for children. Only play this game, if you feel comfortable reading about gore & voilence.
